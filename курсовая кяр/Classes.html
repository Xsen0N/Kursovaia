Our courses require a huge variety of activities: felting, sculpting , ceramics , oil and watercolor painting and much more. Here you can find everything about what is written and even more. More uncertainty with creative activities is not a problem, if you do not like one type of art , you can easily choose something else without wasting time searching.

In addition, every first lesson in a new field is absolutely free.

At first glance, it may seem rather strange that there is such a variety of different activities, but the whole secret is in experienced and skillful teachers who will help you discover yourself in different areas, and they themselves love to learn something new, because art is not stands still, there is always room for improvement.
